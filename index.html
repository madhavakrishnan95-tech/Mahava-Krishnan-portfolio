Research & Selected Projects

Climate, Environment & Urban Resilience

Urban Resilience through Sponge Parks (Master's Thesis, IIT Delhi)

GIS-based SMCA framework using weighted, multi-criteria indicators to prioritise Nature-Based Solutions (NbS) infrastructure sites across Chennai. Integrated spatial, socio-economic, and hydrological data to produce evidence-based planning recommendations for urban flood management.

Landslide Susceptibility Modelling, Uttarkashi

Multi-layer GIS risk zonation combining topographic, hydrological, and land-use indicators to generate a quantitative susceptibility index for climate-driven geohazard risk.

Chennai Floods Impact Assessment

Assessed climate-driven flood risk using spatial and socio-economic data, examining vulnerability of urban communities and infrastructure to extreme precipitation events.

Strategic Foresight & Technology Governance

Food Security Futures 2050

PESTLE-based strategic foresight exercise examining long-term trajectories of climate change, technological disruption, and geopolitical shifts on global food systems. Developed scenario pathways and policy recommendations across a 25-year horizon, covering international governance, trade, and resource security dimensions.

ONDC Digital Commerce & Platform Governance Study

Mixed-methods primary research on India's Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) using semi-structured interviews with two MSMEs (Aruvi Eco Organics and Bhooshan Enterprises). Assessed the Beckn protocol as a model of Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI), with analysis of platform governance, algorithmic accountability, equitable access, and technology sovereignty implications.

DESMAP Spatial Equity Framework

GIS-based analysis assessing equity in digital and physical infrastructure allocation using multi-layer spatial indicators for participatory planning.

Governance, Institutions & Social Policy

Delhi Water Crisis Policy Analysis

CBI Institutional Drift Analysis
